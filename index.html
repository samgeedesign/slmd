Our Courses

South London Music Days are run by a team of professional musicians with a wealth of experience in a variety of areas of the music profession – teachers, soloists, conductors, adjudicators and examiners – and have worked together running popular music courses for children for many years.

We welcome students of all standards, so if you play any instrument come along and have some fun working with like-minded musicians, as well as learning new skills.

Our courses also feature unique experiences including conducting workshops, handbell ringing, and choir, alongside training in performance techniques and stagecraft.

As an exciting recent addition to our courses, we have introduced solo performance workshops for students, providing an opportunity to receive constructive feedback in an informal setting from our instrumental specialists. Students can either play a fully-prepared piece or something they are currently working on.

These 3-day courses form an excellent opportunity to hone performance techniques, in particular the vital skill of working as part of a team. The most important thing for us tutors is to see students enjoying making music in an informal setting and making friends for life as they come back year after year.
